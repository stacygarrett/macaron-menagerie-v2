the Macaron Menagerie

Here at the Macaron Menagerie, we crave the delicious smells of freshly baked confections wafting from our ovens.

Chocolately, cinnamony, buttercream sweetness; lemony, fruity, and always delectable!

With a vast array of flavors to choose from, your taste buds will never get bored.

We first opened our doors in May of 2016 in our humble shop located in SLC, Utah. Now we are spreading the joy of delicious baked goods to all of you throughout the Continental United States.
